A. Confidential Information

In this Agreement, "Confidential Information" means information designated by the Collapseless team or its affiliates as confidential to an individual, or which, by its nature and the circumstances of disclosure, should be treated in good faith. Confidential Information includes, but is not limited to, any discussions and materials between the Collapseless team or its affiliates and individuals relating to unpublished Collapseless products, and the terms of any Collapseless team agreement or protocol template. However, confidential information does not include information that an individual can conclusively demonstrate that:
[I] the individual has not entered the public domain in breach of any obligation owed to Collapseless;
[II] INFORMATION THAT AN INDIVIDUAL RIGHTFULLY RECEIVES FROM A THIRD PARTY THAT IS NOT SUBJECT TO CONFIDENTIALITY;
[III] INFORMATION THAT THE INDIVIDUAL KNEW PRIOR TO FIRST RECEIVING FROM Collapseless and that is not subject to restrictions on use and disclosure;
[IV] INFORMATION DEVELOPED INDEPENDENTLY BY INDIVIDUALS. "Affiliate" of a party to this Agreement means any entity that directly or indirectly controls, controls or is under common control with that party.

B. limit

1. From the date on which Collapseless discloses confidential information to an individual, the individual shall not disclose this confidential information to a third party, otherwise voluntarily withdraw from Collapseless team on behalf of the individual and process it at the same time.
2. Individuals may not reverse engineer, disassemble, or decompile any Software disclosed to Individuals under this Agreement.

C. ELSE

1. All confidential information is the property of Collapseless and may not be disposed of privately.
2. Subject to the limitations contained herein, this Agreement shall inure to the benefit of and be binding upon the parties, their respective successors and assigns.
Collapseless management protocol

This Agreement is entered into by the Collapseless team and the person or entity you fill in in the field in the process of completing this Agreement.

A. obey

In this Agreement, "obey" refers to the restraint and management of the joined individuals by the Collapseless team, and the individuals shall endeavor to obey and enforce the orders if the order is not excessive.

B. quit

In this Agreement, "Withdrawal" means a request to opt out from an individual who has joined the Collapseless team.
1. A response will be received within 48 hours of the request, and the individual has withdrawn by default when the reply is received.
2. After exiting, you cannot join the team again, but the signed agreement is still in effect and will be ignored by the team when the application is sent again.

C. ELSE

1. The Collapseless team does not have the correct working position and time, and Collapseless has no right to interfere with the individual's daily life, but Collapseless has the right to order the individual during the individual's work.
2. Individuals have the right to make suggestions and deficiencies to the team, which will be corrected after review.
